Welcome!
Hello! Welcome to your ACL Rehabilitation improvement program :) This device tracks your movement to monitor your balance and sway. Seen in the "Pateint" tab, the plots represent your balance comparing your injured leg versus your non-injured leg. The greater deviation you sway around the origin (0,0), the larger the range of data points will be plotted on the graphs. The closer the data collected is to the origin, the closer you are to a stronger and improved ACL. You will be indicated when the standard deviation of your data becomes more narrow and that you have improved since your last session of completing the exercises. The exercises you will complete are included in the device manual.There is also a tab for your clinician to look at your performance which will display a variety of plots for them to analyze. Please complete each exercise as indicated on both legs. The goal of this device is to help you with your rehabilitation process in regaining all range of motion in your injured leg.
